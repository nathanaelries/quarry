# Quarry Art Bible

**Project:** Quarry
**Current Version:** 1.0
**Date:** July 10, 2026
**Art Direction Focus:** 1960s Sci-Fi Movie Poster / Comic Book Style

> **Scope note.** "2D-first" here means the *visual style* — the flat, graphic
> 1960s-poster / comic look and a 2D concept-art workflow — not 2D gameplay. Quarry itself is
> the existing **3D, first-person** prototype ([prototype/](../prototype/README.md)); this
> bible is the aesthetic that game will be rendered toward. Ships are a planned *future*
> addition on top of the on-foot core, not a replacement for it.

---

## 1. Art Direction Overview

**Quarry** is a 2D game featuring biomechanical ships fused with Kabbalah mysticism, set
inside and around a massive O'Neill Cylinder.

The art direction prioritizes **strong visual identity** over production polish in the early
stages. All future art, UI, VFX, and level design will inherit from this bible.

**Core Identity:**
**Living Sacred Technology** — biomechanical vessels and structures that feel both mechanical
and alive, adorned with glowing Kabbalistic symbols, sacred geometry, and divine energy.

---

## 2. Visual Style

**Primary Reference:** Iconic **1960s sci-fi movie poster and comic book art**

- Bold graphic composition
- Dramatic angles and strong silhouettes
- Vibrant, saturated colors
- Retro-futuristic aesthetic
- Painterly with clean graphic design elements
- Heroic scale and sense of wonder

**Key Influences:**
- Classic 1960s American sci-fi illustration (movie posters & pulp comics)
- Dynamic lighting and cosmic backgrounds
- Stylized realism with graphic punch

## 4. Core Aesthetic

**"Living Sacred Technology"**

- **Biomechanical fusion**: Organic curves, bone-like structures, glowing energy veins
  combined with mechanical plating and machinery.
- **Kabbalah Mysticism**: Glowing sacred geometry, Tree of Life motifs, ethereal
  Hebrew-inspired runes, divine light emanation.
- The technology should feel **alive** and **ancient** rather than cold and sterile.

---

## 5. The Cylinder (O'Neill Colony)

**The Cylinder** is a massive rotating O'Neill Cylinder space habitat.

### Visual Goals
- **External**: Long cylindrical structure with alternating land strips and large window
  sections. Dramatic scale and retro engineering.
- **Internal**: Curved artificial world where the ground wraps upward. Forests, cities, and
  atmosphere follow the inner curve of the cylinder.

The Cylinder should feel both **technological** and **mystical**, with biomechanical and
sacred geometry elements integrated into its structure.

---

## 6. Biomechanical Ships

The player's ships (and other vessels) are the main expression of Quarry's core identity.

### Design Pillars
- Organic + mechanical fusion
- Bone-like growths and living structures
- Glowing Kabbalistic symbols and sacred geometry
- Strong, readable silhouettes (important for 2D gameplay)
- Retro-futuristic 1960s poster energy
